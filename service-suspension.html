Common

Understanding suspension geometry can be complex. Here are some answers to frequent questions about our tuning and installation process.

Ask something else
What brands do you recommend for daily driving vs track?
For daily driving with occasional spirited runs, we recommend BC Racing or ST Suspensions for a balance of comfort and performance. For dedicated track use, we lean towards KW V3s, Ohlins Road & Track, or Bilstein PSS10s for superior dampening control and thermal stability.
Do I need an alignment after installing coilovers?
Absolutely. Changing ride height directly alters camber, toe, and caster settings. Driving without an alignment will destroy your tires rapidly and negatively affect handling. We include a precision alignment with all our suspension installation packages.
How long does an air ride installation take?
A standard kit installation typically takes 3-4 days. Custom hard-line trunk setups can take 5-7 days depending on the complexity of the design and materials used. We prioritize clean wiring and leak-free reliability over rushing the job.
Will rolling my fenders crack the paint?
Tony is an expert at fender rolling. We use heat guns and a gradual rolling technique to minimize stress on the paint. While there is always a minor risk depending on the age and condition of your paint, our success rate is extremely high for crack-free results.
